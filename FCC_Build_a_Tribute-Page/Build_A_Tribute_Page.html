Michael Jackson

King of Pop

Here's a time line of Michael Jackson's life:

  • 1958 - Born in Chicago.
  • 1964 - Joined the Jackson Brothers—a band formed by their father and which included brothers Jackie, Tito, and Jermaine.
  • 1975 - The Jackson 5 signed with Epic Records, a subsidiary of CBS Records, and renamed themselves the Jacksons.
  • 1982 - The sixth album, Thriller was released, which earned Jackson seven more Grammys and eight American Music Awards.
  • 1984 - Jackson was diagnosed with vitiligo, which Taraborrelli noted may be a consequence of skin bleaching.
  • 1988 - The Bad World Tour began on September 12. Jackson broke a Guinness World Record when 504,000 people attended seven sold-out shows at Wembley Stadium.
  • 1988 - Jackson released his only autobiography, Moonwalk, which took four years to complete and sold 200,000 copies.
  • 1989 "King of Pop" popularized by Elizabeth Taylor when she presented him with the Soul Train Heritage Award.
  • 1992 - Founded the Heal the World Foundation.
  • 1994 - Married Lisa Marie Presley, the daughter of Elvis and Priscilla Presley.
  • 2001 - His tenth solo album, Invincible, was released.
  • 2009 - Jackson held a press conference at London's O2 Arena to announce a series of comeback concerts titled This Is It.
  • 2009 - Jackson died after suffering cardiac arrest.
